Intro

 Games have always been a major source of entertainment in humans. From simple games like Catch, to more complex games like Baseball. Each game provides their own fun to people in differing ways. In schools, games are used as a fun way to help memorize details and help de-stress students from stressful school life. As time went on, things got more advanced. Now games of today prefer a digital medium, but it doesn't seem like schools are taking advantage of the digital medium.

Thesis

Video games need to be utilized more in schools as a tool for learning as they would be a great benefit to teachers and students.
